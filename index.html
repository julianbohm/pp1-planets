Awesome Facts about our Solar System

Planets Facts

Mercury

The smallest planet in the solar system, and also the closest to the sun, is a rocky world, without air and its interior hides a large metal core. The side that faces the sun is very hot, while the other side, where it is at night, is freezing cold. Mercury is one of the 5 planets that can be seen with the naked eye, in its case we can see it before sunrise and shortly after sunset for ancient cultures its movements were fast and confusing compared to the other stars. And that is why it was related to a god. In mercury the sun hides and returns and rises on the same horizon shortly after.

Venus

The hottest planet in our solar system. Venus is the second closest planet to the sun. It rotates on itself slowly and in the opposite direction to the rest of the planets, a day on Venus lasts 243 Earth days and a year on Venus lasts 225! Its day is longer than the year! It is a rock world with a size similar to the earth, but without seas or oceans, the atmosphere of Venus is so thick that it traps too much heat from the sun, so the planet heats up like a furnace and does not cool down, day or night! it is so hot there the iron would melt.
